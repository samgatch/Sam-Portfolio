About Me

Hi my name is Sam Gascho and I'm a 22 year old trying to make a jump into the career I've always been encapsulated with. Since I was young, I've always had a love for all things electroning. After spending 2 years as an electrical engineering major unforseen circumstances have caused me to move home and not finish my degree. After two years of self taught learning I've decided to put myself on the market for getting a foot into the web development field.

2018: The Start

In highschool during one of my engineering classes I discovered a love for all things computers. Whether it was learning different software, building my own computer, or just working on projects through a computer. I fell in love

2020: First Steps

In 2020 I started my degree at Baylor University. While my focus was in computer engineering, I found that I had a knack in learning how to program. The first language I ever learned was MATLAB, soon after that I tried my hand in C++. This was the first real challenge.

2022: A Change

During 2022 a large change happened in my life causing me to move back home. I thought this was the end for my dreams of getting into the tech field.

2024: Where I'm at

Since my leave from school I've still been working on my computer science skills. I did however switch to HTML,CSS,and javaScript. I found that making websites from scratch was fun and entertaining. I'm to the point now where I'm starting a family and want to try my hand a making a career in tech.
